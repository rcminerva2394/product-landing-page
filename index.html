All about fair trade

Join the business revolution

CCAP is composed of a non-government organization(NGO) and a marketing company that empower thirty-one (31) community-based marginal producer groups located in eleven (11) provinces of the Philippines. The said groups employ a total of around nine-hundred (900) artisans. We are a guaranteed member of the World Fair Trade Organization (WFTO) and dedicated to promote Fair Trade.

Empowering poor artisans

All of the products are made by the marginalized and small producers from the Philippines.

Fair payment

The artisans and producers receive fair pay that is mutually negotiated and agreed by all

Respect for environment

We maximize the use of raw materials and with the least overall impact on the environment
