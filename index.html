CCAP is composed of a non-government organization(NG0) and a marketing company that empower thirty-one (31) community-based marginal producer groups located in eleven (11) provinces of the Philippines. The said groups employ a total of around nine-hundred (900) artisans. We are a guaranteed member of the World Fair Trade Organization (WFTO) and dedicated to promote Fair Trade.
